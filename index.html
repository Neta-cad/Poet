About Me

I am Akingboye Ayomide Mishael, a Junior Data Analyst based in Lagos, Nigeria with over 3 years of growing passion for data analytics and database systems. I am currently a student at the National Open University of Nigeria (NOUN), where I am deepening my technical foundation.

I hold a Data Analytics Certificate from Quantum Analytics and have hands-on experience building production-grade PostgreSQL databases deployed on Supabase. Through the Remote Hustle Competition, I advanced to the final stage by building two fully functional analytical database systems from scratch.

I believe the difference between a business that grows and one that struggles is often just good data. My mission is to help organizations make smarter decisions by turning their raw data into clear, actionable intelligence.
